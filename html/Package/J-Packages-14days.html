  • Day 01: Arrival in Colombo
    • Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo.
    • Meet and greet by our local representative.
    • Transfer to your hotel for check-in.
    • Half-day sightseeing in Colombo, visit the city's landmarks.
    • Overnight stay at your hotel in Colombo.
  • Day 02: Colombo to Kandy
    • After breakfast, proceed to Kandy, the cultural capital of Sri Lanka.
    • En route, visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
    • Check-in to your hotel in Kandy.
    • Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic.
    • Evening leisure in Kandy.
    • Overnight stay in Kandy.
  • Day 03: Kandy
    • Breakfast at the hotel.
    • Explore the Royal Botanical Gardens.
    • Visit Peradeniya.
    • Stroll around Kandy Lake and the Kandy Market.
    • Attend a cultural dance show in the evening.
    • Overnight stay in Kandy.
  • Day 04: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya
    • Breakfast at the hotel.
    • Drive to Nuwara Eliya, also known as "Little England."
    • En route, visit a tea plantation and factory.
    • Check-in to your hotel in Nuwara Eliya.
    • Explore the cool, misty city.
    • Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.
  • Day 05: Nuwara Eliya to Ella
    • After breakfast, head to Ella.
    • Enjoy the scenic train journey or take a private vehicle.
    • Explore the stunning Nine Arch Bridge.
    • Check-in to your hotel in Ella.
    • Overnight stay in Ella.
  • Day 06: Ella
    • Breakfast at the hotel.
    • Hike to Ella Rock or Little Adam's Peak for breathtaking views.
    • Explore Ella's charming town.
    • Relax and enjoy the serene atmosphere.
    • Overnight stay in Ella.
  • Day 07: Ella to Departure
    • Breakfast at the hotel.
    • Depending on your departure schedule, you may have some free time.
    • Proceed to Bandaranaike International Airport for your onward journey.
